Refund and Dispute Policy for Online Conference Tickets

Effective Date: 12/22/2024

Thank you for purchasing a ticket to our online conference. Please read this policy carefully, as it outlines the terms and conditions regarding refunds and disputes for ticket purchases made through our website.

1. Refund Policy

1.1 Eligibility for Refunds

  • Refunds will only be issued under the following conditions:
    • The conference is canceled by the organizer.
    • Technical issues caused by the organizer prevent access to the event.

1.2 Non-Refundable Situations

  • Tickets are non-refundable if you:
    • Choose not to attend the event for personal reasons.
    • Encounter technical issues on your end (e.g., device compatibility, internet connectivity).

1.3 Refund Request Process

  • If you believe you are eligible for a refund, submit a request via email to IntentionalCommunityConference​@proton.me within 14 days of the event date.
  • Include your ticket confirmation number and a detailed explanation of your refund request.

1.4 Refund Timeline

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days through the original payment method.

2. Dispute Policy

2.1 Resolution Process

  • If you experience an issue related to your ticket purchase or the event, contact our support team at IntentionalCommunityConference​@proton.me before initiating a dispute with your payment provider.
  • We aim to resolve all issues promptly and fairly within 5 business days of receiving your inquiry.

2.2 Stripe Dispute Handling

  • If a dispute is filed with Stripe without prior communication with our support team, we reserve the right to provide evidence of the transaction and event details to Stripe to resolve the case.
  • Chargebacks deemed fraudulent or unfounded may result in the forfeiture of the ticket and possible restriction from future events.

3. Ticket Transfer Policy

  • If you cannot attend the event, you may not transfer your ticket to another individual.

By purchasing a ticket, you agree to this Refund and Dispute Policy. For further questions or assistance, please contact our support team.
